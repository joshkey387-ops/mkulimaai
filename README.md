
# MKULIMA AI

## Overview
MKULIMA AI is a cutting-edge agricultural technology platform designed to assist farmers in optimizing their productivity through the use of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to empower farmers with the tools and insights they need to make informed decisions and boost their yields.

## Features
- **Crop Management:** Monitor crop health and growth stages.
- **Pest and Disease Detection:** Identify pests and diseases using image recognition.
- **Weather Forecasting:** Get accurate weather predictions linked to farm locations.
- **Soil Health Monitoring:** Analyze soil conditions and receive recommendations.
- **Market Insights:** Access market trends and pricing information.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Cloud Services:** AWS
- **AI and ML:** TensorFlow, Scikit-Learn
